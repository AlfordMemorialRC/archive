76 Repeater
146.760 - PL 107.2

 

The 76 Repeater was replace several years ago, and the receive antenna is approximately 100 ft up the Channel 8 TV transmission tower. The transmit antenna is under the pyramid roof on top of the public observation building. The repeater is now a Kenwood commercial grade repeater and is controlled by an SCOM 7330 controller.
